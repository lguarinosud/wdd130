York, England

York is a historic city located in northern England, known for its well-preserved medieval architecture and rich cultural heritage. The city is surrounded by walls and boasts numerous landmarks and attractions such as York Minster, a grand Gothic cathedral, and the Shambles, a narrow street with timber-framed buildings. York also has a vibrant cultural scene with various museums, galleries, and theaters, and is famous for its traditional afternoon tea and iconic Yorkshire pudding.
