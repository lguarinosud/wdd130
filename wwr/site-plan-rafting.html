Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website for the white water rafting company is to attract potential customers by showcasing the thrilling experience and expertise of the guides, provide detailed information about the trips, facilitate easy booking, build trust and credibility with customer testimonials and safety information, and engage with customers through a blog, social media integration, and newsletter signup.

Audience

Who are the target customers? The people who go to the white water rafting company are usually people who love outdoor activities and want to challenge themselves physically. They can be alone or with others like families, friends, or people from work. They are usually between 18 and 45 years old and have enough money to pay for an exciting and safe outdoor adventure. They like outdoor activities, nature, and sports. What do they want? They want an exciting and safe rafting trip led by knowledgeable guides. They want to experience the thrill of the rapids and enjoy nature while feeling secure. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? By accessing the website, customers expect to find information on different rafting trips, pricing, equipment, safety, and guides' qualifications. They may also have questions about the local area and other outdoor activities available. What are their needs that aren't being met? The customers' needs that may not be met include a lack of knowledge about the area and rafting experience, trust in the company's safety measures and qualifications, or difficulty finding a reliable and experienced rafting company. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Customers may access the website mainly using desktop computers and laptops ahotugh not limited to them, they may also use smartphones, or tablets.
